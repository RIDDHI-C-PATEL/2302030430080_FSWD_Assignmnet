SEO Tips & Tricks

What is SEO?

Search Engine Optimization (SEO) is the practice of optimizing your website to rank higher in search engine results pages (SERPs). This improves the visibility of your website to users searching for relevant terms.

Why is SEO important?

SEO helps in driving more traffic to your website organically (without paid ads). Higher ranking means more visitors, which can lead to better engagement and conversions.

SEO Best Practices

Here are some key SEO best practices to follow:

  • Use descriptive and keyword-rich titles and meta descriptions.
  • Optimize images with alt text.
  • Ensure your website is mobile-friendly.
  • Improve page speed.
  • Use internal links to enhance navigation.
  • Create high-quality, engaging content.
  • Focus on acquiring high-quality backlinks.
